Paycoinshares is a suite of financial tools within a protocol built on top of the Paycoin Blockchain.

Production

We are targeting the creation of 1,000,000,000,000 XPS through the process of burning Paycoins. Burns are validated by sending Paycoins to provable unspendable address during special periods of time known as Burn Cycles.

Security

XPS is a special share unit available to everyone. It is a surrogate of Paycoin that shares the features and qualities of the underlying XPY asset.

Sustainability

Interaction with common hardware achieves the realization of an environmentally friendly platform. Plans for inflation and various components of economics are set to track that of Paycoin growth.

Speed

Real world applications naturally demand realtime drivers of events. Paycoinshares is no exception and delivers on its promise of instant transactions. Most network confirmations occur within 1 minute and trades in the billions of units happen flawlessly.
