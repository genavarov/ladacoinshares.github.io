Reddcraft is a suite of financial tools within a protocol built on top of the Reddcoin Blockchain.

Production

We are targeting the creation of 1,000,000,000,000 XRT through the process of burning Reddcoins. Burns are validated by sending Reddcoins to provable unspendable address during special periods of time known as Burn Cycles.

Security

XZR is special share unit available to every person on the planet. It is a surrogate of Reddcoin that shares the features and qualities of the underlying RDD asset.

Sustainability

Interaction with common hardware achieves the realization of an environmentally friendly platform. Plans for inflation and various components of economics are set to track that of Reddcoin growth.

Speed

Real world applications naturally demand realtime drivers of events. Reddcraft is no exception and delivers on its promise of instant transactions. Most network confirmations occur within 1 minute and trades in the billions of units happen flawlessly.
