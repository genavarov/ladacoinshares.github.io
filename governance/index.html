Reddcraft Governance

Production

In line with historical production goals of mass produced items, between a 5 and 15% increase over target will be place into effect to provide enough units for unforseen events. Additional and essentially independent units may be created within the platform.

Authority

Reddcraft is guided and influenced by its core developers and stakeholders. We strongly believe in the principle of transparency and provide everything about the suite and its data publically. Everyone is allowed insight in manner that is safe for the market participants.
